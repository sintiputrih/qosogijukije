Flashback-Free Beauty Products: Powders, Foundations, Concealer

In the age of Instagram and Snapchat, it makes sense that we want to be camera-ready all the time. But we are also in the age of high-definition finishing powders and holographic highlighters and sometimes our flawless glow doesn’t translate well under those flashing lights. If you’ve ever experienced dreaded “flashback,” like Angelina Jolie did back in 2014, then you know the struggle.

Stylish believes that no one and nothing should take away your shine, and luckily there are plenty of beauty products that practically guarantee nighttime snaps that won’t make you look like a ghost. Bright white powders, concealer that’s a shade too light and HD powders, while perfect for daytime, can cause flashback when a camera flashes in the dark. Fortunately, we’re here to help.
