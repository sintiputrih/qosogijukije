Career

Rita Ora started her career by performing at her father’s pub in London occasionally. In the year 2008, she has auditioned for the popular television reality show titled Eurovision: Your Country Needs You and got selected to represent a British contestant for the Eurovision Song Contest 2009. She later withdrew her name from the competition as she thought she was not still ready for that challenge. In the same year, she signed a record deal with the American label Roc Nation.

In the years 2009 and 2010, she made a cameo appearance in the videos titled Young Forever by Jay Z and Over by Drake. In the year 2011, she released covers and videos on her YouTube channel regarding the release of her debut album. In the year 2012, she has been featured in the single titled Hot Right Now by DJ Fresh as a female vocalist.

In the same year, she has released two popular singles titled RIP and How We Do Party from her debut album. Later in the same year, she released her debut album titled Ora. She also embarked on her first headlining tour to promote her debut album in the United States.

She has released another single titled Shine Ya Light in November 2012 and has been featured as a special guest in the concert for the 100th Anniversary of the Independence of Albania at Tirana, Albania. In the year 2013, she embarked on her first UK tour titled Radioactive Tour also in support of her debut album. In the year 2014, she has released two singles titled I Will Never Let You Down and Doing It from her second studio album.

In the year 2015, she has split from the Roc Nation label and signed a new record deal with Atlantic Records and Warner Music Group in the year 2016.

In the year 2017, she has released her first solo single titled Your Song. In the same year, she released another single titled Anywhere. In the year 2018, she released the song titled For You for the movie titled Fifty Shades Freed.
