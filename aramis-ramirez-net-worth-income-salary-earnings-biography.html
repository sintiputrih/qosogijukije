Aramis Ramirez Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Aramis Nin Ramirez was born on June 25, 1978, in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic, and is a retired professional baseball third baseman who played for the Pittsburgh Pirates (1998-2003, 2015), Chicago Cubs (2003-2011), and Milwaukee Brewers before retiring in 2015. He spent 18 seasons in Major League Baseball (MLB), with the Pirates (1998-2003, 2015), Cubs (2003-2011), and Brewers (2015-present). Aramis has been in three All-Star games throughout his career, in 2005, 2008, and 2014, and was the winner of the Silver Slugger Award in 2011.
