Rachel Skarsten is a Canadian actress who is best known for playing the role of Dinah Lance in the American TV drama series, Birds of Prey, as well as her role as Tamsin in the supernatural drama series, Lost Girl. Rachel was also cast as Elizabeth I of England, a role which brought her many positive reviews, in the historical romantic drama series, Reign. She has also been a part of various other TV shows and movies such as Jack Brooks: Monster Slayer, Virginia’s Run, American Pie Presents Beta House, Two Hands to Mouth, Fifty Shades of Grey, Molly’s Game, Angels in the Infield, Category 7: The End of the World, Made… The Movie, The L.A. Complex, Transporter: The Series, Beauty & the Beast, In My Dreams, Wynonna Earp, Marry Me At Christmas, and Imposters. Rachel Skarsten also has an adventurous streak and loves traveling a lot. Rachel comes from a very well-educated family and she herself is someone who takes education very seriously. She has also garnered a major social media fan base with her acting finesse and has more than 100k followers on Twitter and more than 350k followers on Instagram.

Education

From grades 4 to 8, Rachel Skarsten went to the Claude Watson School for the Arts and majored in Visual Arts and the Cello. She also took lessons at the Royal Academy of Dance for 12 years. Then, from grades 9 to 12, she studied in Claude Watson Arts Program at Earl Haig Secondary School and majored in Visual Arts in the top 10% of her class and was also acknowledged as an Ontario Scholar.

Skarsten also has a double degree in English Literature and Classical Studies which she attained from Queen’s University in Kingston, Ontario.
