Shaquille O’Neal Net Worth & Income

One of the greatest basketball players of all time, Shaquille O’Neal’s net worth is a whopping $400 million as of December 2023.

The primary source of income for the living legend currently is his business ventures, endorsements, and acting.

Astonishingly Shaq earned a total of $292 million in NBA salary alone. Similarly, endorsements during his playing career brought more than $200 million.

Even though he has been retired for years now, he makes more money than what he earned in his 19 years of playing career.

Shaq bags about $60 million from various endeavors in a typical year. That’s enough money to make him one of the highest-earning celebrities in the world.

During his peak, he earned more than $29 million in salary per year, which is so much more than what some of the fortunate people make in a lifetime.

NBA Contracts

Shaq signed an entry-level contract with Orlando Magic in 1992 for four years.

As per the contract, he earned a yearly salary of $4.35 million, making a total of $17.4 million throughout his time with the team, which expired in 1995.

Similarly, after his time with Orlando Magic, he signed a contract with Los Angeles Lakers for seven years in 1996.

The contract was worth $120 million with an average yearly salary of $17,142,857.

He was at the peak of his career at the time. So Lakers were not on the verge of losing their star player.

They extended the contract with Shaq for three years following the expiration of the previous one.

The extension contract was worth $88.5 million with a yearly salary of $29.5 million.

Moreover, due to the rising tension between the fellow teammates, i.e., Shaq and Kobe, the Lakers traded Shaq to Miami Heat in 2005.

Shaq signed a contract with Heat in 2005 for five years, which was worth a staggering $101 million.

During his tenure with the Heat, Shaq bagged about $20.2 million in yearly salary.

The contract came to an end in 2009.

After that, Shaq inked a deal with the Boston Celtics for two years which was worth $27.51 million with a yearly salary of $1.37 million.

Sadly he retired from NBA in the year 2011.

However, between 2009 and 2010, he played for two more teams for a brief time in the NBA, namely Phoenix Suns and Cleveland Cavaliers.

His time with Phoenix Suns earned him $41 million in total, and Cleveland Cavaliers earned him $20 million.

Apart from NBA, Shaq makes money from the multiple investments he’s made over the years.
