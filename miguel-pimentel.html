Early Life

Miguel Pimentel was born on October 23, 1985, and is currently 38 years old. He is from San Pedro, California, USA. His full name is Miguel Jontel Pimentel.

As of 2021, he is 36 years old and his birth sign is Scorpio. Talking about his family background his father is Mexican American and his mother is African American.

His nationality is Mexican-American. Sadly, his parents divorced when he was 8 years old. Then, he and his brother lived with their mother.

He, as a child, listened to R&B, funk, hip-hop, rock, and jazz, mostly due to the diverse musical tastes of his parents. Likewise, by the age of 14, he was writing and recording his songs.

Career

Miguel is a singer and actor by profession. In 2000, he signed a production deal with ‘Drop Squad’. Similarly, in 2004, he was signed by the independent record label Black Ice.

In 2006, he completed his first studio album ‘Young & Free’. ‘Black Ice’ delayed the album, releasing only the single “Getcha Hands Up”. Not satisfied with the single, its video, and his treatment overall, he parted ways with the label.

Since then, he has stated that this experience taught him to take control of how he was marketed, rather than relying on the label to differentiate him from the crowd of male R&B vocalists.

In the year 2007, he signed a new recording contract with ‘Jive Records’.

Likewise, his next album ‘All I Want Is You’ was not released for several years pending a lawsuit brought by Black Ice for breach of contract. And, the album was released in November 2010.

All I Want Is You eventually spent 45 weeks on the Billboard charts and sold more than 400K copies in the USA.

He has also ventured into the movies. The movies are ‘Live by Night’ (2016). There, he starred as Esteban Suarez. He also appeared in ‘Detroit’ (2017) as Malcolm.

Likewise, he starred in the series ‘The Platinum Life’ (2017) as Himself. And, his voice acted in the movie ‘Fearless’ (2020) as Arcanist.

Other Albums

In 2007, he signed with Jive Records and recorded his debut album All I Want Is You. Its release was put on hold for three years when Black Ice sued him for breach of contract.

After his legal troubles were sorted out, he released a mixtape and wrote songs for other Jive artists, including Usher, Musiq Soulchild, and Asher Roth.

The lawsuit was finally settled in 2010 and All I Want Is You was issued in November 2010. Sadly, it was not as much of a success as he had hoped for.

Although, its title track got a certain amount of airplay and was enough to earn him a spot as a supporting act on tour with Trey Songz and Usher.

And, the follow-up singles titled “Sure Thing” and “Quickie,” peaked at No. 1 and No. 3 on the Billboard R&B/Hip-Hop Singles chart, respectively.

Also, the runaway hit track, “Adorn,” achieved regular radio and video play. “Adorn” was compared to Marvin Gaye’s “Sexual Healing” for its sensual tone and easy pace.

Also, Miguel Pimentel won the ‘Soul Train Music Award for best R&B/soul male artist and received several additional nominations throughout the season.

Furthermore, in the year 2015, he released his next album ‘Wildheart’. Later, in the year 2017, he released ‘War & Leisure’. It had hit songs such as “Sky Walker” and featured artists like Rick Ross and Travis Scott.

Awards

In the year 2012, he won the award ‘Best Collaboration’ for “Lotus Flower Bomb” with Wale. For his2013s work ‘Himself’, he won the award ‘Best Male R&B Artist.’

Followingly, in 2013, his “Adorn” won the Grammy Award for the ‘Best R&B song’. In 2011, for his work in ‘Himself’, he won the award ‘Best New Artist.’

And, in 2012, for his work in ‘Himself’ and ‘Tonight (Best You Ever Had)’, he won the awards ‘Best R&B/Soul Male Artist’ and ‘The Ashford & Simpson Songwriter’s Award’ respectively.

Also, for his work in ‘Himself’, he won the award ‘Best R&B/Soul Male Artist’ in 2013.

Personal Life

Speaking of his personal life, Miguel Pimentel is a married man. He has been in a relationship with model Nazanin Mandi since 2005. It was announced in January 2016 that they were engaged.

Therefore, they married on 24th November 2018 at Hummingbird Nest Ranch in Santa Susana, California, USA. The couple first met on the set of Miguel’s first music video, “Getcha Hands Up”.
