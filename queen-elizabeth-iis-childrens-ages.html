Queen Elizabeth IIs Childrens Full Names &amp; Ages

Queen Elizabeth II died on 8th September, 2022 at the age of 96 at Balmoral at her royal castle in Scotland just a year after her husband, Prince Philip also died at the age of 99.

She is said to be the only British monarch who ruled on the throne for 70 years. The late Queen Elizabeth had four kids with her husband; King Charles III, 73; Princess Anne, 72; Prince Andrew, 62; and Prince Edward, 58.

King Charles III

The current King of Britain, King Charles III is Prince Philip and Queen Elizabeth’s first son. He was born on 14th November, 1948. King Charles is 73 years of age as of 2022. He has a total of two children, Prince William, who is 40 years and Prince Harry, 37 as of 2022 with his ex-wife, Princess Diana. Princess Diana and her husband, Prince Charles were married from 1981 to 1996.

After she died from a car accident in Paris, Prince Charles remarried in 2015 to Camila, Duchess of Cornwall. He was the first in line for the British throne after the death of Queen Elizabeth II. Now, he’s king and his wife is his queen escort.

Princess Anne 

The only daughter of the queen, Princess Anne is the late Queen Elizabeth’s second child. She was born on 15th August, 1950. She is 72 years of age as of 2022. In the year 1973, she tied the knot with her first husband, Olympic gold medalist, Mark Philips.

They were married for about 20 years until they divorced in 1992. They shared only 2 kids, Zara, 41 years and Peter, 44. She married her second husband, Sir Anthony Lawrence the same year she divorced her first husband.

Prince Andrew

Queen Elizabeth II’s third child, Prince Andrew is the late queen and late Prince Philip’s third child. He was born on 19th February, 1960. Prince Andrew is 62 years of age as of 2022. He is a father of two beautiful daughters called Beatrice, 34 years and Princess Eugenie, 32 years as of 2022. Prince Andrew shared these children with his first wife, Sarah, Duchess of York. He and his ex-wife were married from 1986 to 1996.

Prince Edward 

The queen’s last born, Prince Edward is Prince Philip and Queen Elizabeth’s last born. He was also born on 10th March, 1964. Prince Edward is 58 years of age as of 2022. He has two kids with his wife, Sophie, Countess of Wessex.

Their kids’ names are Lady Louise Windsor, 18 years and Viscount Severn, 14 years of age as of 2022. Presently, Prince Edward is 12th in line for the British throne.

Queen Elizabeth Children In Order

The late Queen Elizabeth II had four adorable children with her late husband, Prince Charles. In order of birth, King Charles III is the first, followed by Princess Anne, then Prince Andrew and finally Prince Edward.

Who Is Queen Elizabeth II’s Favorite Child?

Without being biased, it is factual to say that, the late Queen Elizabeth II also had a favourite child out of her four children. According to the royal biographer, Prince Andrew is the late Queen Elizabeth II’s favourite child despite Prince Charles being the successor of the throne.
