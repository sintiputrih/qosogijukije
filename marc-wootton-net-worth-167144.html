About

Famous for his portrayal of fraudulent psychic Shirley Ghostman in the BBC3 comedy series High Spirits with Shirley Ghostman. He is also well known for his role as The Glaze on Adult Swim's Delocated and for his portrayal of Cozzo on the English sitcom Way to Go.

He rose to fame through his appearances on the Channel 4 comedy program The Eleven O'Clock Show and also made early-career appearances in the mockumentary film Confetti and on the game show My New Best Friend.
