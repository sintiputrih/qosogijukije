Robert Fishman Found Dead in Old Fort Bayou

Near the Old Fort Bayou mouth on Friday morning, a man from Ocean Springs was discovered deceased.

Robert Fishman, 39, has been named as the victim by Jackson  Coroner Bruce Lynd. Around 8 a.m., he was discovered in the ocean.

Around 10:30 p.m. on Thursday, the Department of Marine Resources received a report about an unmanned boat in Old Fort Bayou. Fishman’s body was ultimately discovered there as well.

According to DMR officials, Fishman may have been the only person in the boat when it collided with the underwater object. And that he was probably thrown into the water by the force of that crash.

Fishman, according to Lynd, was not donning a life jacket.
