Jay Leno Net Worth : Career &amp; Lifestyle

An American standup comedian, actor, writer, producer, and former late-night television host, Jay Leno’s net worth is $450 million. 

Probably the man with the best car collection globally, Jay Leno is known as a talk show host of NBC’s The Tonight Show with Jay Leno.

The show aired from 1992 to 2009.

Leno was inducted into the Television Hall of Fame in 2014.

Leno also writes in Popular Mechanics and gives automotive advice.

Jay Leno, originally James Douglas Muir Leno, was born on April the 28th, 1950, in New Rochelle, New York, United States.

His mother, Catherine, was a homemaker and was born in Greenock, Scotland. She came to the United States when she was 11 years of age.

Similarly, his father Angelo was born in New York City and sold insurance policies for a living.

Leno’s paternal grandparents were Italian immigrants from Flumeri, Italy.

Leno was raised in Andover, Massachusetts, and attended Andover High School. After graduating from high school, Leno went to Emerson College and obtained a bachelor’s degree in speech therapy.

Soon after college graduation, Leno started a comedy club in 1973.

His older brother worked as an attorney and was also a Vietnam War veteran.

McLaren F1

2006 EcoJet

According to hot cars, this piece of engineering is priceless.

Leno’s EcoJet is a supercar that can be best described as a scientific showcase.
