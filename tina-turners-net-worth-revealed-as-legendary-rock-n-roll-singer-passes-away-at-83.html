Tina Turners Net Worth Revealed as Legendary Rock n Roll Singer Passes Away at 83

One of the greatest vocalists and entertainers in the world, Tina Turner, has gone away. After a protracted illness, the Queen of Rock ‘n’ Roll passed away on May 24, 2023, in her home in Küsnacht, Switzerland, not far from Zurich.

“Tina Turner, the ‘Queen of Rock’n Roll’ has died peacefully today at the age of 83 after a long illness in her home in Küsnacht near Zurich, Switzerland,” said a press release from her UK representative, Bernard Doherty.

In his closing remarks, Doherty said, “With her, the world loses a music legend and a role model.” On Wednesday, the singer’s social media accounts also posted the tragic news of her passing.
