The Hasty Mint (Japanese: せっかちミント Hasty Mint) is an item introduced in Generation VIII. It is one of the types of mints that change the effect of a Pokémon's Nature.

In the core series games

Price

Effect

If used from the Bag, it changes the effect of a Pokémon's Nature on its stats to that of the Hasty Nature, increasing its Speed stat and decreasing its Defense stat.
