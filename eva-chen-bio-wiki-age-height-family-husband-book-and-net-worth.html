She is currently head of social media platform fashion partnerships and has launched a number of fashion and e-commerce features at Instagram, including shopping fashion, looks directly from influencer feeds, and the Instagram Shop account.

    Chen is also an author of the book and has written three children’s books, all illustrated by Derek Desierto: Juno Valentine and the Magical Shoes in 2018, A Is for Awesome: 23 Iconic Women Who Changed the World in 2019, and Juno Valentine and Fantastic Fashion Adventure in 2019.

    Eva Chen Juno Valentine and the Magical Shoes

    Chen published Juno Valentine and the Magical Shoes with Macmillan imprint Feiwel & Friends in November 2018, a fairy tale-slash-fashion handbook about a young girl, Juno Valentine, who is on a magical journey to find her shoes lost. To do this, she tries throughout history on pairs belonging to famous women — such as Serena Williams, Jane Austen, Misty Copeland, Cleopatra, and Frida Kahlo — and is transported into their worlds.
