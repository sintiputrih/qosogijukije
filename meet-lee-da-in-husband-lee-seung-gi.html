Meet Lee Da-in husband, Lee Seung-gi

Actress Lee Da-in, real name Im Joo-hee, was born in Seoul, South Korea, on November 5, 1992.

Her parents are Lee Hong-heon and Kyeon Mi-ri. Her sister Lee Yu-bi and she were both adopted by their parents.

Lee’s last name, which was originally Lee Joo-hee, was changed to Lee Ra-yoon prior to getting married. Her stage name remained the same, though.

With the tvN drama “Twenty Years Old,” Lee Da-in made her acting debut in 2014.

She continued to perform in numerous dramas, including Make A Woman Cry, Entourage, Hwarang: The Poet Youth Warrior, My Golden Life, Come and Hug Me, Doctor Prison, and Alice.

Currently, she is a part of the cast of the upcoming drama Lovers, which also stars Namgoong Min, Ahn Eun Jin, and Lee Hak Joo.

Lee Da-in takes on the role of Kyung Eun Ae, the fiancee of Nam Yeon Jun (Lee Hak Joo) and the closest friend of Yoo Gil Chae (Ahn Eun Jin).

Who is Lee Da-in’s husband?

Lee Da-in has a relationship with actor Lee Seung-gi. On February 7, 2023, Lee Seung-gi confirmed their engagement in letters posted on his social media platforms.

They married on April 7, 2023, in front of friends and family at the Grand Intercontinental Seoul Parnas.
