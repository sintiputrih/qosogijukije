Minori Suzuki

Minori Suzuki (Japanese: 鈴木みのり Minori Suzuki) is a Japanese voice actress for the Pokémon anime.

Biography

Suzuki was born in Aichi Prefecture, Japan on Oct 1, 1997. Her voicing acting career started in Macross Delta. She went with and got picked out of 8000 people, before this she had done no voice acting work. She had her first voicing role in the main series anime as Regina, eventually getting a major role voice acting Liko from Pokémon Horizons: The Series.
