Jay Mohr Parents: Meet Jon Wood Mohr, Jean Ferguson

Jay Mohr is an American actor, comedian and radio host.

The comedian first garnered the spotlight while a cast member on Saturday Night Live (1975), where he got recognised for his impersonations of people such as Sean Penn and Christopher Walken.

In September 2015, he began hosting Jay Mohr Sports, a daily midday sports radio talk show on Fox Sports Radio but left the outlet in January 2016 to pursue acting ventures.

Mohr has starred in several films and tv shows including Jerry Maguire (1996), Suicide Kings (1997), Picture Perfect (1997), Paulie (1998), Mafia! (1998), Small Soldiers (1998), Are We There Yet? (2005), Street Kings (2008),  Ghost Whisperer (2006–2008), Gary Unmarried (2008–2010), The Cleaning lady (2022), et al.

Jay Mohr Parents: Meet Jon Wood Mohr, Jean Ferguson

Jon Wood Mohr and Jean Ferguson are the parents of Jay Mohr.

Jay’s mother Iva Jean Ferguson was a nurse, and Jon Wood Mohr was a marketing executive.

Jon Wood Mohr and Jean Ferguson share three children; Julia Mohr, Jay Mohr and Virginia Mohr.
