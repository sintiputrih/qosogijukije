'Fit to Fat to Fit' Trainer Goes Shirtless After Weight Gain

Taking sympathy weight to the extreme! Fit to Fat to Fit’s Tuesday, February 16, episode includes trainer Corey Taylor stunning his client with his new body, as seen in Us Weekly‘s exclusive sneak peek.

The A&E unscripted series focuses on personal trainers putting on weight in order to understand how to best help their clients. In the clip, a shirtless Taylor meets up with Raela after not having seen her for four months, during which time he has voluntarily gained 56 pounds. His diet: doughnuts, pizza, burgers, fried pickles and quesadillas. 

“You look different,” she tells her trainer with a laugh. She then says to the camera, “I had to do a double take. My eyes were glued to his stomach for about a good five minutes.”

“I knew he was going to gain weight, but it wasn’t anything that I imagined at all,” Raela continues. “It’s a big motivation for me.”
