See Kylie Jenner Pose in a Sultry Green Bandeau Bikini

Kylie Jenner just debuted a new, jaw-dropping bikini look.

This afternoon, the Kylie Cosmetics founder shared yet another set of swimsuit pics, in which she poses while sitting by the pool in a luxe cushioned wood chair. The star's tiny olive-green two-piece by Sommer Swim is comprised of tiny string bottoms and a tie-front bandeau top, featuring ruched detailing, long trailing strings, and a circle peekaboo feature at the center. She elevated the suit with an array of gold accessories, including a dainty belly chain, a stack of bangles, and layered pendant necklaces.

For the casual day, the beauty mogul kept her makeup minimal with a nude lip and dark lip liner, while wearing her hair in loose waves.

She captioned the pics, "happy saturday."

Kylie Jenner//Instagram

Kylie Jenner//Instagram

Last month, the reality star marked the beginning of bikini season in a set of fiery pics, where she sunbathes on a lounge chair outside what appears to be older sister Kendall Jenner's home. The younger Jenner wore in a fiery red string triangle bikini from FWRD, and accessorized with a pair of gold huggie hoop earrings and a dainty gold anklet.

When she's not in a bikini, Jenner often graces us with her enviable street style. On a recent trip to Paris, the star stepped out in a string of elegant, monochromatic looks, including a white halter midi dress from Alaïa and romantic black halter dress, courtesy of Bottega Veneta. She also recently embraced the cottagecore trend, in a June Instagram post where she posed in Dôen's dreamy smocked Stanza dress.
