Kaitlyn Bristowe's Wine Line Has Already Sold Out

Another reality star is joining the bustling wine biz! Kaitlyn Bristowe, who was the star of the eleventh season of The Bachelorette, recently announced that her own line of wines is now available, slightly ahead of schedule.

Though the alcoholic beverages won’t officially go on sale until later this month, the former Bachelor contestant simply couldn’t contain her excitement. “I could no longer wait … so my wine is now available for pre-launch on my website,” Bristowe, 33, wrote on Instagram. “For all you ordering! You will receive it mid-June! So not TOO long of a wait.”

The line, which is called Spade and Sparrows, currently consists of three wine varietals that have been “hand-selected” by the reality star. Each wine has both nights in and evenings out in mind, and they all cost around $20 per bottle, not including shipping.

The rosé has hints of rose petals as well as “added flavors of wild strawberries, apricots and white raspberries.” The pinot gris, on the other hand, boasts notes of citrus blossom, lime zest, white peach and honeydew, creating what is described as an “explosion of flavors.” Last but not least, the cabernet sauvignon is “bold and mysterious” thanks to a flavor profile that includes vanilla, cocoa dust, black cherries and sugar plum.

However, if any of those wines sound good to you, you’ll have to wait a bit longer before you can enjoy any of them. Though Bristowe announced the pre-launch just days ago, all three wines have already sold out. When one fan lamented on Twitter that she waited too long to order Bristowe’s creation, the Canada native said she would be “restocking soon.”
