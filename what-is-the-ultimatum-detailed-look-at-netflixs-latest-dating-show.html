What Is 'The Ultimatum'? A Detailed Look At Netflix's Latest Dating Show

Another day, another dating show! Netflix is set to premiere yet another dating show called The Ultimatum: Marry or Move On, and this one is a little different from most.

Netflix does have another show called The Ultimatum that originally premiered in 2009, so don't get that confused when this dating show comes to the streaming platform next month. The official trailer dropped, and it already looks like things are going to get heated.

Dating shows get everyone talking and become addictive. The platform is no stranger to these types of shows as it is the host to Love IsBlind, Too Hot To Handle, Dating Around and more. Find out why people can't stop talking about this new provocative dating show.

Will this show be your new obsession? Here's what we know about the new Netflix dating show, The Ultimatum: Marry or Move On.

5 When And Where To Watch 'The Ultimatum: Marry Or Move On'

As we said before, The Ultimatum: Marry or Move On will premiere on Netflix with a start date of April 6. There will be ten episodes in total, but the good thing is you won't have to wait long to watch them. The first eight episodes drops on that day, but then the finale and reunion will be released on April 13.

4 What Is 'The Ultimatum: Marry Or Move On' About?

The Ultimatum has a very interesting plot line. Most dating shows take singles and place them all together in order to find a relationship, but not this time. No, this show takes a couple that is already in a relationship- one of them wants to get married, and the other doesn't and places them in a house with other couples in the same situation. According to the Netflix description, "an ultimatum is issued—and in just over eight weeks, they must commit to marriage, or move on. In the meantime, each will choose a new potential partner from one of the other couples, in a life-changing opportunity to get a glimpse of two different possible futures."

3 Who Is Hosting 'The Ultimatum: Marry Or Move On'?

If you are a fan of dating shows, you might recognize these familiar faces. Nick and Vanessa Lachey are back to host yet another dating show. The couples also hosts Netflix's Love Is Blind, which just wrapped up its third season. In the promo on their Instagram, Nick explained that Vanessa gave him an ultimatum, after they had been dating for five years, so they about this all too well.

2 Who Is On 'The Ultimatum: Marry Or Move On'?

Netflix has not released any of the details about the cast yet, but we do know there are six couples who will all be compatible with multiple people in the house. However, there are plenty of scenes from the trailer that gives viewers a glimpse into the dilemmas they will be facing. The trailer opens on a woman saying, "The only thing scarier than losing you right now would be to marry you." While a male cast member seems to be changing his mind. "My eyes have opened up to a lot of things that I want in a relationship," he said.

1 What Are Fans Saying About 'The Ultimatum: Marry Or Move On'?

Of course, fans are already weighing in when it hasn't even premiered yet. And the response is mostly positive. Most people on Twitter are saying that even though the show looks like trash, to "count them in" on watching it. Others are saying they can't wait to watch it because the Lacheys "have a chokehold" on them. People just can't seem to stay away from dating shows, especially ones that will cause dramas.
