How Old is Galina Becker and Where was She Born?

Galina Joelle Becker, a former athlete, was born on March 11, 1987, in Florida, United States. This makes her 36 years old this year. She’s the third child of her parents, Mildred and Kevin Becker.

The popular wrestler’s wife has two elder sisters, Undine Becker and Malisa Cooper. Galina is an Afro-American, meaning she has a black origin. There aren’t many details about her family background. Roman Reigns’ wife developed an interest in athletics at a young age. This set the pace for her career.

She was an Athlete in High School 

Galina Becker grew up in San Francisco, California, alongside her older siblings. According to sources, her oldest sister, Undine, a sports athlete, inspired Galina’s love for sports. She attended high school at Mt. Pleasure High School in San Jose. There, she exhibited exceptional sporting ability in hurdles and high jump.

She participated in several competitions like long and triple jump events, clinching the award for the Most Valuable Performer in her school team. Her sporting prowess continued throughout her sophomore, junior and senior years. She holds the title for the Central Coast Section record in the triple jump, scoring the 40 ‘5” mark.

Besides track and field events, the fitness model also performed excellently in volleyball.  Following her graduation from high school, Galina proceeded to the Georgia Institute of Technology to bag a degree in Management.

Galina Becker and Roman Reigns were College Sweethearts

Roman Reigns and Galina Becker met as students at the Georgia Institute of Technology. While Reigns was part of the Georgia Tech Yellow Jackets football team, Galina was involved in track and field events. The duo became an item and they were always attending each other’s games. The lovebirds started dating in 2008, and they’ve been inseparable since then.

Galina Becker Became Roman Reigns’ Wife Following a Private Wedding Ceremony  

Roman and Galina got engaged on February 26, 2012, and tied the knot in 2014, two years after their engagement. The celebrity sweethearts had dated for over 5 years before getting married. The famous wrestler and his belle married on a private island called Castaway Cay in the Bahamas. Only their friends and family attended the Samoan-themed wedding ceremony.

It was reported that the theme song for their wedding was a soundtrack from a Disney Movie – A Whole New World”. By the time they wedded, Roman had become a WWE superstar with some titles to his name.

Galina and her husband have been married for 9 years. The duo are ever supportive of each other, and it shows in their disposition towards their careers and family life. Galina stood by her husband in difficult times of his career and when he was diagnosed with leukemia. Their relationship has stood the test of time.

The secret to their long-lasting relationship is that the couple understands each other. This is depicted in how they manage their kids. In an interview, the professional wrestler revealed that he and his wife delegate tasks, schedule and prioritize time with their children.

He further divulged it is loud and hectic when you have many kids, and it is a must to schedule and get everything in line. He further added that to them, it is almost like a business and he and his wife have to delegate and give all their children the much-needed attention they deserve.

How Many Children Does Galina Becker Have With Roman Reigns?

Roman Reigns and Galina Becker have 5 children together. They had their first daughter, Joelle, also called Jojo, in 2008. Jojo was born out of wedlock when Roman Reigns’ wrestling career was about to kick off. The couple welcomed twin boys in 2018, two years after their marriage.

In 2020, another set of twins was added to the family. Galina and her husband seldom share information about their children on social media. Thus, we have little or no information about the children’s exact date of birth and their names. However, it’s deduced from Galina’s Instagram page that she has two daughters named Anoai and Sapphire.

Is Roman Reigns’ Wife Also a Wrestler?

No, she’s not. While Galina is keen on sports, she delved into fitness modeling at a point in her career. After graduation from college, the beautiful mother of 5 carved a niche for herself in the modeling world.

Boasting a well-toned physique, Galina soon started getting photoshoot contracts, including a lifestyle photo session with Michael Krinke. According to Michael, Galina is one of the best models he’s worked with. Shortly after giving birth to her daughter, she decided to take a break from her modeling career and focus on her growing family.

What Does Roman Reigns’ Wife Do for a Living?

Presently, Galina is a homemaker who strives to be the best mother to her children while throwing her weight behind her husband. The celebrity star used to be very active on social media, tweeting about WWE and connecting with other WWE wrestlers. After the birth of her twins in 2016, Galina took time off social media. It’s believed that she wants to focus on parenting.
