What paint did Van Gogh use?

Apr 25, 2024 Van Gogh primarily used oil paint for his artworks. He favored this medium due to its flexibility and vibrant colors, which allowed him to achieve the expressive and textured brushwork for which he is famous. Oil paint consists of pigments mixed with a drying oil, typically linseed oil, and it dries slowly, allowing artists to work with the paint for longer periods and make revisions as needed. Van Gogh experimented with various brands and types of oil paints, using both commercially available tubes and hand-ground pigments mixed with oil.
