Camryn Bridges Bio (Wiki)

Who is Camryn Bridges?

Camryn Bridges is an American dancer and reality TV personality. She is known for her appearance in ‘Dance Moms’.

Who are the parents of Camryn Bridges?

Camryn Bridges was born Camryn Nevaeh Bridges on May 11, 2002, in St. Louis, Missouri, US. As of 2023, her age is 20. She was born to parents Camille Bridges (mother) and Ryan Bridges (father).

Camryn is one of her parents’ three children. She has a sister named Ryleigh and a brother named Ryan Jr. Nevaeh Bridges.

She spent her childhood days in St. Louis, Missouri, US. Her love for dancing developed from a young age. Her ethnicity is African-American.

Education: School/College, University

Talking about her education, Camryn Bridges learns dancing at B. Elite Dance Academy in St. Louis, Missouri.

Camryn Bridges: Professional Life, Career

In 2015, Camryn Bridges made her TV debut by guest-starring in the TV series, ‘JoJo’s Juice’. The next year, she made an appearance in the reality dance competition show, ‘Dance Moms’ which increased her popularity significantly.

She made her first appearance in season 4. Later, she performed in the seventh season as a solo dancer and also gave a number of incredible group performances.

The lyrical dance routine, ‘We Go On is one of her best group performances in which she danced alongside the likes of Nia Frazier, Kalani Hilliker, Brynn Rumfallo, Kendall Vertes, and Daviana Fletcher.

Bridges were first invited to join the elite team after being called back from the Dance Moms and Abby’s audition process.

Eventually, Camryn Bridges became a permanent member of the show until ‘Ashlee’s Big Decision Part 1’ in which she and some other great dancers left the ALDC and formed ‘The Irreplaceables’.

In 2017, Camryn Bridges was featured in Kendall’s music video for ‘Where Would I Be Without You.

Apart from being a dancer, Bridges also does modeling. She has modeled ‘True Joy Active Wear’ which was founded by her mother and AUDC winner McKaylee True.

Although she is great as a group dancer, her mother wants to make her a great soloist.

Camryn Bridges has her own self-titled Youtube channel which she launched on November 3, 2014. As of May 2020, she has over 101k subscribers on her channel and her video views have crossed 5.9 million views.
