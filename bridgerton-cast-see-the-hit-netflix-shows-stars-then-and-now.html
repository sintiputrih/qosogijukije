Bridgerton Cast: See the Hit Netflix Show's Stars Then and Now

Credit: Netflix (2)

Luke Newton

Newton has delivered a memorable performance as the third eldest Bridgerton son over the years. Before taking over as the season 3 lead, Newton returned to his theater roots in the 2023 revival of The Shape of Things in the U.K. 

The actor credited Page and Bailey for helping pass the Bridgerton torch. 

“[Page] sort of grabbed me, gave me a big hug and was like, ‘Are you ready for this, bro?’” Newton recalled in an April 2024 interview with InStyle about Page's suggestion that he enjoy a vacation before diving into season 3.

Newton praised Bailey for being there for him the "whole" time, adding, “We talk all the time, and it was really nice in season 2 seeing how he conducted himself on set, knowing that I’d be next. It was nice to see someone do it so well.”
