A 15-year friendship

Kreischer has been a regular guest on 'JRE' since the podcast's inception. It has aired over 2000 episodes so far.

Kreischer has disclosed his acquaintance with Rogan on several occasions. He would often tell the story of how he believed Rogan to be gay. He discussed it with Logan Paul on 'IMPAULSIVE' and with fellow comic Andrew Schulz.

“When Joe wanted to be friends with me, I was like, ‘You must be gay’. Like, why would you wanna be friends with another man? That didn’t make sense to me,” he said in an 'IMPAULSIVE' episode. “Why would you wanna hang out with him? And then Joe never tried to kiss me once.”

Kreischer has also shared how Rogan and Ari Shaffir helped him through his dark moments. It is hard to picture Kreischer and Rogan on the outs, despite the possibility of a tiny friendly feud.
