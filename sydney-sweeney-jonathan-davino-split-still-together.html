Are Sydney Sweeney And Jonathan Davino Still Together?

It’s been a long time since the Euphoria star Sydney Sweeney and her boyfriend Jonathan Davino was last spotted together. They seemed to have gone private with their relationship and even refrained from talking about each other over interviews.

So, what was going on? Are they still together? Or have they split already? Find answers as you scroll down. 

Are Sydney Sweeney And Jonathan Davino Still Together? Have They Split?

According to the Jan 2022 reports, Sydney Sweeney and Jonathan Davino were still together.

The couple first came to the spotlight back in October 2018 after sharing dinner at Spring Place for InStyle and Kate Spade. Since then, they were often spotted together on numerous occasions including Jonathan’s birthday in 2019 at TAO Chicago nightclub.

But it has been quite a while since we last saw them in July 2021 while on a beach vacation in Italy. The lovebirds were captured getting cozy on a boat, and bathing under the sun at the time.

Naturally, fans would be curious if the two have split. Moreover, Sydney shared with EliteDaily that she was “with a friend” during her beachside vacation, and this raised a lot of questions.

But while a few may have trusted Sydney, many were quick to point out that they don’t kiss their friends like that. They believed that the Euphoria star was just trying to throw people off the scent.

And to talk about “throwing people off the scent”, Sydney’s a master at it.

Back when her first PDA pictures with Jonathan surfaced in 2020, a fan reacted with a tweet, “Sydney Sweeney has a man… thanksgiving canceled.” Surprisingly, she responded to the tweet sharing a photo of herself at prom with a girl, captioned, “baby girl go have a feast. your girl at prom ;).”

Fans were in uproar then. “Sydney Sweeney likes girls too… I have to lay down for 85 hours just to process this um,” a user wrote.

Also, another tweeted, “I don’t think I realized Sydney Sweeney isn’t straight this is the best day of my life.”

In the end, fans were arguing about whether she liked girls or not, instead of discussing her relationship with Jonathan. Well done, Sydney.

When Did Jonathan Davino And Sydney Sweeney Begin Dating?

As mentioned before, it was October 2018 when Jonathan Davino and Sydney Sweeney first came to the limelight after sharing dinner at Spring Place for InStyle and Kate Spade. So, it was probably sometime before that the two began dating.

However, fans only became sure of their relationship in November 2020. It was after her PDA on the beach and her IG posts with her dog Tink. Yes, don’t be surprised. It wasn’t her pics with the dog, rather fans were intrigued about the person who took these photos.

But as to the date of writing this article, neither of the lovebirds had revealed how or when they first met.

What’s more, Jonathan had yet to make his appearance on Sydney’s IG @sydney_sweeney then.

Meet Jonathan Davino, Sydney Sweeney Boyfriend

Jonathan Davino was born in 1983, making him 14 years younger than Sydney. This Italian man co-owned the restaurant called Pompeii Xpress at 2931 N. Broadway Ave in Lakeview along with his father Roger.

Reportedly, the pizza company “Pompei” was founded by Luigi Davino in 1909. Over time, the family business from just a small bakery specializing in bread and limited pans of pizza turned to the quick service Italian Restaurant it is today.

But the franchise was owned by his uncle, Ralph Davino, and Jonathan had opened a branch without his permission. Thus, sued by his uncle later Jonathan was forced to change the restaurant’s name to Davino’s Xpress.

Talking about his family, Jonathan is the son of Roger and Velda Davino. Some of his siblings and cousins’ names included Kimi, Tommy, Mark, Carissa, Robert, Jimmy, and Anthony.

Jonathan is a hard man to find. Unlike his girlfriend who flaunted a massive fan following, he had no social media presence.

Sydney Sweeney Dating History

Sydney Sweeney is hesitant about sharing her current dating life, let alone her past.

This American actress was born on September 12, 1997, in Spokane, Washington, USA, and made her debut in the movie ZMD: Zombies of Mass Destruction as “Lisa” at the age of 12.

Since then, Sydney has appeared in over 40 movies and TV series including the popular Grey’s Anatomy (2005), 90210 (208), Pretty Little Liars (2017), In the Vault (2017), Everything Sucks! (2018), The Handmaid’s Tale (2018), and The White Lotus (2021).

Recalling her journey, Sydney shared, “I grew up in a family that when we moved to Los Angeles, the prices were so different from where I grew up in Spokane, Washington.” It seems that her parents struggled financially and even had to file for bankruptcy when she was a teenager. 

But things are different now. Sydney flaunts a net worth of $ million as of 2022, and her parents are living the dream. Moreover, Sydney’s brother Trent Sweeney is also an actor/assistant director, making his parents the proudest.
