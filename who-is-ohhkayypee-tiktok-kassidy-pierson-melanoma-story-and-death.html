Who is Ohhkayypee TikTok? Kassidy Pierson Melanoma Story And Death

Kassidy Pierson aka Ohhkayypee is a TikTok user who raises cancer awareness. We have to share this article about her Story and Death

Kassidy Pierson noticed a mole on her inner thigh when she was 18 years of age. The mole was dry, itchy, and sometimes bleed.

But she never thought of taking it seriously and have a check-up. She later shared the picture of her mole and talked about its seriousness.

Who is Ohhkayypee TikTok?

Ohhkayypee was just a normal TikTok user till she discover she had cancer. She had a lot of followers in TikTok.

Kassidy, used her TikTok account by the username @ohhkayypee. Mostly her posts were related to her health updates and her experience.

She shared the details about the skin cancer that she is fighting. It was her thoughtfulness that while in the terrible condition she thought of sharing it.

She had chosen TikTok to document her battle with Melanoma and hoped if it could help some others with similar cases.

Kassidy accumulated 1.3M likes over her TikTok videos and 230.7K followers. She put the status ‘currently dying’ in her TikTok account.

Isn’t it ironic? how a person can countdown her life especially when she is young, beautiful, married, and got a lovely kid.

It must be indeed a painful journey to her. Yet, she managed to keep herself motivated and thought of educating others.

Kassidy Pierson’s Melanoma Story & Death

She was not aware that her symptoms of mole were indicators of Melanoma. She was pregnant at that time and didn’t have health insurance.

Kassidy finally got her insurance at 21 and took the biopsy. Two weeks later she came to know that she has skin cancer. And it was a serious one.

It was widely spreadable and a life taking cancer. She underwent various surgeries. There are three types of skin cancer.

Out of three, melanoma is considered the most dangerous due to its ability to spread throughout the body, rest are called basal and squamous cell carcinoma.

She fought bravely till melanoma had taken her life on September 8, 2021. Kassidy Pierson died of skin cancer.

How old was Kassidy Pierson’s Melanoma?

Kassidy Pierson is 27 years old. At the early age of 18, she had symptoms. But unfortunately, it was too late for her to get diagnosed.

She preferred to raise awareness so others could be benefitted. She had shared a photo of the cancerous mole that led to her diagnosis.

This urges her TikTok followers to have their skin checked.

Meet The TikToker Husband and Baby?

The TikTok user was married and got a son named Hunter. Her pregnancy prevented her from having a check-up.

She shifted her focus on her unborn baby. Only almost after 3 years of having a baby, she got her insurance and stated her skin was examined.

She was prepared to embrace the death and she wrote letters, made videos, and got gifts for her son so he would reminiscent her even in her absence.
