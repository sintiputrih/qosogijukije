Is Brooke Anne Smith Married? Does She Have A Boyfriend?

Brooke Anne Smith has been cast in General Hospital. It led fans to asking if the beautiful actress is married. If not, who is her boyfriend? The Colorado native has been working in the TV shows for a long time and fans are also interested in her love life.

Read all about her in this article below.

Is Brooke Anne Smith Married?

No, Brooke Anne Smith is not married as of July 2023. In December 2016, she posted a pic while donning a diamond ring and quoted Ralph Waldo Emerson, “The hues of the opal, the light of the diamond, are not to be seen if the eye is too near,” in the caption.

However, she also confirmed that she wasn’t engaged in the caption as she wrote, “#ThoughtForTheDay SIDE BAR- NOT ENGAGED, just like the photo and the quote 🤗❤️💋.”

Does Brooke Anne Smith Have A Boyfriend?

Brooke Anne Smith is very reserved about her dating life. The actress is very active on social media. She also frequently posts about her family members and her cast on the platform. But in the plethora of social media posts, she has never posted about her dating life or hinted at being in a relationship, or any sign of the actress having a boyfriend.

Hence, there isn’t much to go on but to conclude that she is likely single as of July 2023.

How Old Is Brooke Anne Smith?

Born in 1984, Brooke Anne Smith is 39 years old.

Brooke Anne Smith Family

Brooke Anne Smith is one of the three children of Patricia “Patty” Smith and Thomas Smith.

Thomas, by trade, is an airline pilot/captain, per the Father’s Day post from June 2013. On Father’s Day Post in 2012, Brooke wished her father, “Happy Father’s Day!! <3 My dad!!!.” Likewise, on Mother’s Day 2013, the actress wished her mom, “I have the best mama on the planet!!! Mom, you are beautiful inside and out and I am so lucky to be your daughter. Love you more than anything. ❤❤❤.”

In 2023, Thomas reached 73 years old.

His wife Patty, who celebrates her birthday on 27 May, is 75 years old. She attended Albany High School and Valdosta State University.

Thomas and Patty also have two sons from their marriage. Their son Thomas Jr. is currently serving in the US Army. On the Veteran’s Day post, Brooke posted a pic of her brother and captioned it, “Today & every day we should remember those who have fought for our beautiful country and for those who currently brave the fight for freedom. Love to all Vets!!! ❤️❤️.”

On his birthday in March 2015, Brooke wished her brother, “#HappyBirthday #LoveYou SO much! He reminds me to soar, to never give up, and that we can do anything we set our minds to. #Proud.”

Thomas Jr. is 35 years old as of July 2023.

Whereas, her other brother Scott Smith 42 years old. He attended Smoky Hill High School and studied Aeronautics at the Metropolitan State University of Denver. Scott followed in his father’s footsteps and became an Airline Pilot at FDX and also works as an entrepreneur.

Scott is married to his wife Stephanie Smith (@stephie2172). The married couple celebrated their 7th wedding anniversary in September 2022.

In February 2023, Scott posted on his Facebook, “Proud of my little sis! Check out her latest flick Corsicana!”

How Much Is Brooke Anne Smith Net Worth?

The concrete figure for Brooke Anne Smith is unclear but it has to be somewhere below $1 million.

The American actress is best known for her lead series regular role in two seasons of Tyler Perry’s “Too Close To Home” where she played a drug-addicted sexual assault survivor appearing in a total of 16 episodes. She is also known for her recurring role on MTV’s hit show “Awkward.”

Brooke landed a lead series regular role in Oscar winner Renee Zellweger’s six-million dollar pilot “Cinnamon Girl” which was directed by critically acclaimed Gavin O’Connor. She made her motion picture debut as a lead in Walt Disney’s “Max Keeble’s Big Move” and since then, has gone on to several television shows and feature films to date.
