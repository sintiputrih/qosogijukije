The Hollywood-Loved Evolvetogether KN95 Face Masks Are Back in Stock

Celebrities have donned a handful of eye-catching face masks throughout the pandemic, but only one brand has been spotted again and again on some of Hollywood's most well-known faces: Evolvetogether.

The recognizable masks, which feature global coordinates of various cities, like NYC, Milan, Paris, Tokyo, Santorini, and more, have been worn by everyone, including Jennifer Garner, Sarah Jessica Parker, Angelina Jolie, and Katie Holmes. Even more celebs like Kristen Stewart have recently caught on to the trend, so it's no surprise that Evolvetogether's protective KN95s consistently sell out. If you've been waiting to get your hands on a pack, there's good news — they're finally back, but definitely not for long.

According to the brand, over 500,000 people signed up for the KN95 restock waitlist, so it won't be surprising if they're gone in a flash. Currently, you can snag colors like black, navy, gray, and beige, while other colors like burgundy (which is Garner's recent preferred pick) and white are expected to be restocked soon.

Made with six layers, including a water-resistant exterior, an activated charcoal layer for odor-absorption, and a moisture-absorbing interior, the KN95s have an adjustable nose bridge, soft ear loops, and are made in a factory regulated by the Food and Drug Administration. They also fit the newly updated face mask guidelines from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, which state that well-fitting KN95s and National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH)-approved N95s offer the highest protection to reduce transmission of COVID-19, as opposed to loosely woven cloth options.

The Evolvetogether KN95s are available in packs of five for $15 or variety packs of 15 for $40. You can also opt for its three-ply masks, which are usually more steady in stock and come in similar colors as the KN95s but with different coordinates.

Whichever color or multipack of the Evolvetogether KN95 masks you're eyeing, you'll want to act fast, because they could be gone in the blink of an eye.
