Ibray Seaside Resort And Suite, Satellite Town, Nigeria

Ibray Seaside Resort and Suite is an elegant and classy seaside hotel resort situated in Satellite Island, Ibasa in the Satellite Town area of Lagos State.

Hotels in seaside are generally known for their charm, but Ibray Seaside Resort and Suite sets the bar high with a competitive price and the friendliest service in town. The hotel has everything guests want in a fun-filled vacation or romantic getaway.

The hotel rooms are fully air-conditioned and quite spacious with king-size beds to boot.

Important amenities a modern/standard hotel should have from good water, 24-hour electricity supply, wireless Internet for guests to work and access the Internet in a relaxed and serene environment can be found.

Bar and a restaurant also meet the food and drinks need of guests at the hotel as well as other facilities such as the state-of-the-art swimming pool, cozy sitting area and many more.

At Ibray Seaside Resort and Suite, we look forward to welcoming you to our unique world of hospitality and recreation.
