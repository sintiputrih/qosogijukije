Eva Pilgrim Biography

Eva Pilgrim is an American journalist who was born and brought up in Seoul, South Korea. She currently serves as an ABC News correspondent since December 2015.

Also, Eva has appeared in such shows as “ABC World News Tonight with David Muir”, “Good Morning America” as well as “Good Morning America Weekend Edition”.

Moreover, she works as a reporter and anchor at Action News WPVI since December 2012. Before, Eva worked as an anchor and reporter at WXIN TV from November 2009 to November 2012.

Eva Pilgrim Grandmother

Pilgrim’s maternal grandmother was born and raised in North Korea before the Korean peninsula was divided. She and her younger brother escaped to Seoul when the war broke out.

Her grandmother’s family was left behind in the North while she built a new life in South Korea. Pilgrim, her mother, and her siblings grew up listening to her grandmother’s stories about her life in her old home before the conflict.

Eva said her grandmother dreamed of visiting her old home in the North one day and finding out what had become of her family. She even tried looking for her family in lists and tried sending money and gifts, hoping it would reach her family.

When Eva was in high school, her grandmother was diagnosed with stage 4 stomach cancer. Her mother spent months in Seoul taking care of her.

When her grandmother passed away, Eva’s mother, aunts, and uncles had her cremated as per her last will. They scattered her ashes along the eastern South Korean shoreline, as far north as possible.
