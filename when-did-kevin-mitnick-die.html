Kevin Mitnick Death: When Did Kevin Mitnick Die?: Kevin Mitnick, officially known as Kevin David Mitnick was an American computer security consultant, author, and convicted hacker.

He was best known for a crime spree during the 1990s that involved the theft of computer data and credit card numbers.

Mitnick used his skills to work his way into the phone and cell networks, vandalizing government, corporate, and university computer systems.

For these crimes, he was the subject of a lengthy manhunt by the FBI in the 1990s that turned him into the United States’ most famous cybercriminal.

Following his arrest, conviction, and release in 2000, he began a new career as a security consultant, writer, and public speaker.

Sadly, the famed hacker has died. Kevin Mitnick passed away at 59 on Sunday, July 16, 2023, after battling pancreatic cancer for more than a year.

The news of his demise was made public on Wednesday, July 19, 2023, by a cybersecurity training company he co-founded, KnowBe4.

The news was also confirmed and reported in an obituary posted on the website of Dignity Memorial, a Las Vegas funeral home.
