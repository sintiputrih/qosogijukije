Eleanor Neale - Age, Family, Bio

British YouTube star who has garnered renown for her self-titled channel and its beauty content. She has gained particular popularity for her Get Ready with Me vlogs, makeup tutorials and hauls, and true crime videos. Her true crime videos have often been over an hour long and provide extensive details about famous murders and serial killers. 

Before Fame

She began her YouTube channel in March 2016.
