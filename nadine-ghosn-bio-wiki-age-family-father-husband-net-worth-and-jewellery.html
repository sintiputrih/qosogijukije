Nadine Ghosn’s Father Carlos Ghosn

Ghosn’s father Carlos Ghosn was born on 9 March 1954, in Porto Velho, Rondônia, Brazil. He is known for being the former CEO of Michelin North America, former chairman and CEO of Renault, former chairman of Mitsubishi Motors, former chairman and CEO of Nissan as well as the former chairman of AvtoVAZ.

On 1 April 2017, he stepped down as CEO of Nissan, while still being the chairman of the company. On 19 November 2018, he was arrested at Haneda Airport on allegations of under-reporting his income and abuse of company assets. On 22 Nov. 2018, Nissan’s board reached a unanimous decision to remove Ghosn as Nissan’s chairman.

On 26 November 2018, Mitsubishi Motors’ board also followed by dismissing him. Renault and the French government extended their support to Ghosn at first, considering him innocent until proven guilty. However, they eventually found the situation untenable and Carlos was forced to resign as chairman and CEO of Renault on 24 January 2019.

While free on bail given in early March 2019, Carlos was arrested again in Tokyo on 4 April 2019 over different charges of misappropriations of Nissan funds. Nissan’s shareholders proposed to oust Carlos from the company’s board on 8 April 2019, On 25 April, he was released again on bail.

In June 2019, Renault revealed 11 million euros in suspicious expenses by Carlos, leading to a French probe and raid. With the help of an American private security firm, he escaped from Japan to Lebanon on 30 December 2019, breaking his bail conditions. Interpol issued a red notice to Lebanon seeking Ghosn’s arrest on 2 January 2020. Ghosn is an internationally wanted fugitive to date.
