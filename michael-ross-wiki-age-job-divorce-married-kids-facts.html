Michael Ross is known as the ex-husband of Emmy Award-winning American journalist and television personality Alicia Quarles. Besides, he holds a job in philanthropy for over a decade, getting involved in numerous non-profit organizations.

Michael Ross Wiki; Age, Family, Education

Michael was born in 1984 that makes him 36 years of age. He was raised by American parents in Saint Petersburg, Florida, the United States. However, the information about his family is still inside the dark shadow.

Ross pursued his high school education at St. Petersburg High School in his hometown. After graduating, he went to the University of Southern California to study Psychology. He graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Psychology from USC in 2006.

What Is His Job?

Michael currently holds a job in philanthropy. He now serves as the Director of Individual Giving at City Year, an education nonprofit organization dedicated to helping students in New York. Likewise, he is the Member of Consulting Staff at P.S. 314, inc, which operates at the intersection of philanthropy, social causes, and storytelling. He previously served as the Director of Philanthropy at FoodCorps, which connects kids to healthy food in school. From 2006 to 2008, he served as an Account Executive at Ad Forum Advertising Agency.

Ross began his career in 2006 as a Production Assistant at Rose City Productions. Similarly, he served as the Public Relations Assistant at Dogmatic Production Company. Moreover, his work includes The Association of Community Employment Programs for the Homeless (ACE), Covenant House, and The National Center for Learning Disabilities (NCLD).

Did Alicia Quarles Divorce Michael Ross?

Michael and Alicia Quarles got married after years of being together in 2009. The duo were college sweethearts, who first met while they were studying at the University of Southern California. They dated for five years before deciding to walk down the aisle.

Their marital relationship was going smoothly for years. Alicia revealed that she is very happy with her husband during the 2012 interview with Haute Living magazine. However, the wiki sources suggest that the couple is no longer together. Moreover, it is not clearly known whether the couple is divorced. Alicia is now enjoying her single life according to her Instagram posts.

Do They Share Kids?

It looks like Michael and Alicia do not share any kids despite the years of relationship. Meanwhile, the DailyMailTV’s Senior News Correspondent is a kid lover and she often keeps posting pictures with her sister’s children on Instagram.
