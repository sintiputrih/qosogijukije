Who Was Richard Roundtree?

Richard Roundtree, an esteemed American actor and former model, made history when his portrayal of John Shaft in “Shaft” cemented his place as one of Hollywood’s iconic actors – not only was his portrayal iconic but it became synonymous with Blaxploitation cinema itself.

However, long before Shaft’s soundtrack resonated in theaters, a young Roundtree was making waves in the modeling world. Discovered by Eunice W. Johnson in the early 1960s, his chiseled features and undeniable charisma soon translated perfectly onto the big screen. In his illustrious career, Richard amassed more than 150 acting credits, showcasing an enviable range that extended beyond just action roles.

His battle with breast cancer, a rare diagnosis for men, not only showcased his resilience but also transformed him into an advocate. Using his platform, he actively raised awareness about male breast cancer and even championed for the Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Foundation.
