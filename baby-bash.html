In 2005, Super Saucy was released, its lead single being “Baby, I’m Back”, a collaboration with singer Akon. Cyclone followed in 2007, with its title single featuring T-Pain and its follow-up, “What Is It” featuring Sean Kingston, landing on the Billboard charts as well.
