Yusuke Kozaki - Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokmon encyclopedia

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Yusuke Kozaki (Japanese: コザキユースケ Kozaki Yūsuke; born May 12, 1978) is a Japanese manga artist and character designer. He is the character designer for Pokémon GO. Kozaki has also contributed artwork to the Pokémon Trading Card Game.
