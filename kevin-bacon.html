Kevin Bacon - News &amp; Photos

Kevin Bacon is a Hollywood actor, best known for his roles in movies including Footloose and Tremors. The star is married to fellow A-lister Kyra Sedgwick, and the pair share two children - Travis and Sosie. Like their famous parents, both children have gone on to carve careers out in the entertainment industry - Travis is a musician, and Sosie is an actress. Kevin has worked with his family on a number of projects over the years, most recently starring in Space Oddity in 2022, which Kyra directed and Travis worked on the music for the sci-fi film. Kevin and Kyra split their time between their various home in the United States, including their Connecticut farm, where Kevin often shares fun music videos from on social media with their pet animals.
